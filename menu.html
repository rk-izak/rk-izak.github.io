Tasting Menu

An intimate trio of dishes to savour: from zesty olives and crunchy bruschetta to a comforting risotto of wild mushrooms.

Amuse‑bouche: Citrus Mint Olives

Plump black olives gently marinated in zesty lemon and orange, fresh mint leaves and a hint of cracked pepper.

Starter: Bruschetta

Crunchy slices of baguette brushed with garlic and olive oil, crowned with melted mozzarella, ripe tomato and onion, and finished with basil and chives.

Main Course: Wild Mushroom Risotto

Creamy arborio rice gently simmered with a medley of wild mushrooms, Parmesan and a splash of cream and lemon, finished with fresh parsley.

Your Dining Experience

Begin with citrus‑kissed olives and golden bruschetta to spark conversation. Then settle into the warmth of a creamy mushroom risotto, prepared with love and patience.

Wine Pairing

We recommend a crisp dry white with citrus and mineral notes to complement the creamy risotto, or a light cherry‑forward red with earthy undertones to marry with the wild mushrooms.

Dessert

For the grand finale, the dessert is a surprise — our honoured guest will bring a sweet treat to share.
